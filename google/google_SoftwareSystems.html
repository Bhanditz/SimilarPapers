Software Systems

What We Do

At Google our products require computer systems technology at a scale previously unknown to the industry. At the heart of our business model is an insatiable appetite for compute power. We grow and improve our business by innovating across a spectrum of applied research topics.

Building up from our hardware foundation, we develop systems technologies from across the entire systems stack to address the requirements of cloud computing at Google-scale. We design, build and deploy warehouse- and global-scale network fabrics, with a goal of giving developers the illusion of unlimited bandwidth and microsecond latency. We build storage systems that scale to exabytes, approach the performance of RAM, and never loose a byte. We design algorithms that transform our understanding of what is possible.  We build distributed systems abstractions, towards realizing an environment where Google-scale applications are developed with the ease of a desktop app, and where our developers are the most productive in the industry. We write and publish research papers, because we believe in sharing information, and that the peer feedback and interaction will help us build better systems in the future.
