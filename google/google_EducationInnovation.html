Education Innovation

What We Do

Proposals in the Education Innovation area include innovative uses of:

  • cloud- or mobile-based applications in an educational context
  • educational technology (which is any technology that supports teaching and learning)
  • curriculum in computer science education
The focus is on practical projects that produce tangible results.
